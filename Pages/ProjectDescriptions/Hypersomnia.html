SleepyTime Studio was created by a group of university students with the aim to create and publish a Horror game. We were no more than 9 individuals with a software development background. As a gameplay and asset programmer, I was tasked with designing and implementing the following:
  • Player stats (health, stamina, inventory, etc..).
  • Adding functionalities to all interactable in the game.
  • Blending the main character's animations.
  • Pin Pad Puzzle mechanic.
  • Async scene loader.
  • Pause menu.
  • I have also had the chance to make some 3D models using 3ds Max.
'Waterfall methodologies' were applied during development.
